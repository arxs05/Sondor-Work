About me

I coach you in the "people part of your job! As a coach, I have 20 years of experience working with professionals like you either individually or in (remote) teams, often in intercultural settings. I help you build and maintain dynamic relationships through effective communication and effectively navigating the relationship part of the job.

I have worked with clients in a variety of industries such as high tech, fin-tech, academia, hospitality, and environment. My coaching has been deeply informed by a career in Global Human Resources and living abroad for almost 30 years. I've been trained and certified in many different communication, conflict management and coaching methods to help members with different learning styles get ahead.

My specialties include effective communication, team management, conflict management, change and transition management and coaching introverted professionals.
